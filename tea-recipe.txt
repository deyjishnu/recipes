Ingredients:
 - 1 cup water
 - 1/2 teaspoons tea leaves
 - 1 teaspoons sugar
 - 1/4 cup milk

Directions:
 1. In a bowl, pour the water and add sugar. Heat till it starts boiling.
 
 2. Add tea leaves, and cover the bowl.
 
 3. Let the tea leaves soak for 5 minutes. Heat it again a little.

 4. Discard the tea leaves and serve hot in a cup.
